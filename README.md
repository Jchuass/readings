# ✨ Celestial Fortune

A beautiful, single-file fortune-reading web app. Choose from ten divination paths — from Tarot to BaZi to the I Ching — and receive rich, detailed readings under a starry purple sky, greeted by an angel and a shooting star.

**No installs, no server, no dependencies.** Just open `celestial-fortune.html` in any browser.

## 🔮 Reading Paths

| Path | What you get |
|------|--------------|
| **Tarot** | 5 spreads: single card, Past-Present-Future, Situation-Obstacle-Advice, 5-card Clarity Cross, and the full 10-card Celtic Cross — with reversals, card essences and a spread synthesis |
| **Angel Cards** | Your Daily Angel Card (personalized, one per day), single messages, and two 3-card spreads — each with a practice and affirmation |
| **Numerology** | Expression, Soul Urge and Personality numbers from your name, with love/career/growth breakdowns |
| **Life Path Number** | Life Path, Birthday Number and Personal Year cycle, including master numbers 11 · 22 · 33 |
| **Destiny Code** | The number pyramid hidden in your birth date, layer by layer to your root number |
| **BaZi 八字** | Real Four Pillars calculation — Day Master, seasonal strength, spouse palace, yin-yang and five-element balance |
| **Saju 사주팔자** | The Korean Four Pillars tradition — Ilju, spouse seat and your five-element weather |
| **Zi Wei Dou Shu 紫微斗数** | Simplified Purple Star Astrology — Life, Body and Migration palaces with your main star |
| **I Ching 易經** | Authentic three-coin casting — changing lines, trigram imagery and the transformed hexagram |
| **Compatibility 💞** | Two birth charts woven through seven systems into one score, with strengths, watch-outs and counsel |

## 🌟 Features

- Hand-drawn SVG angel with halo, wings and sparkles, plus an animated shooting star and twinkling starfield
- Genuine sexagenary (stem-branch) calculations for BaZi/Saju — day pillars anchored to the real calendar, solar-term month boundaries, and the verified King Wen hexagram table for the I Ching
- **Export any reading as a PDF** with a clean print-friendly layout
- "Go Deeper" links after every reading to established sites for further study
- Fully responsive, works offline, zero tracking — everything runs in your browser

## 🚀 Getting Started

```bash
git clone <your-repo-url>
cd celestial-fortune
# then simply open celestial-fortune.html in your browser
```

Or host it anywhere static files live (GitHub Pages works out of the box).
